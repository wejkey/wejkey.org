FAQs

Are the plugins compatible with all versions?

No. These plugins are built for versions above 1.20. But some of them may work on lower versions but I can't tell. If you want to try them, It's on you. Support ends here.

How can I install them?

After you click the download button, you will be redirected to GitHub releases where you can click on '(nameofplugin).jar'. After this you need to go to your 'plugins' folder on your server, upload '(nameofplugin).jar' there and start/restart your server.

What are 'dev' versions?

Plugins are under development and they may not work as the stable ones. 'Dev' updates are more frequent and it can take few days/weeks until the stable version is out.

What if I found bug?

You can report the bug on Github Issues page or on Discord server.
